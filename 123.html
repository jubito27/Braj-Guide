राधे राधे!

I am Chitranshu Sharma, a simple sevak born in the holy dust of Shridham Barsana. This is the same land where Shri Radha Rani appeared and gave the true definition of love and devotion to this universe. Born in a Brahmin family, I got the company of culture, Vedas, devotion, and service since childhood. Braj is not just a place for me, it is my life, my faith, and my service area. This feeling inspires me to offer the service of Braj Darshan, information about divine places, and especially Live Darshan to all you devotees. My aim is that those who are far away from Braj, they too can experience the dust, devotion, and leela of this place in their hearts. Jai Jai Shri Radhe!
